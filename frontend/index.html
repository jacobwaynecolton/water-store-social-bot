What this bot does

✍️

Writes captions

Uses Claude AI to write platform-specific captions for Facebook and Instagram, rotating through 9 content themes covering water systems, hot tubs, BBQs, saunas, hydration tips, and more.

🖼️

Generates images

Picks a real photo from your approved photo folder first. If none matches the post theme, it generates a DALL-E image — watercolour style for lifestyle content, infographic style for educational posts.

📸

Auto photo tagging

Drop any photo into data/approved_photos/ with any filename. Claude vision looks at it and figures out what it shows — no renaming needed. New photos are tagged automatically every 6 hours.

📅

Posts on a schedule

Runs 3 times a day by default (9am, 1pm, 6pm). Avoids repeating similar topics back to back by tracking the last 6 posts and telling Claude what's already been covered.

📲

Cross-posts to both platforms

Publishes to the Facebook Page and Instagram Business account simultaneously. Facebook gets a conversational caption; Instagram gets a punchy version with hashtags.

💬

Replies to comments

Checks all posts from the last 7 days every 30 minutes. Any new comment gets a short, on-brand reply written by Claude. Complaints are acknowledged and directed to call or DM.
